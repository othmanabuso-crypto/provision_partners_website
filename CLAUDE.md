# Provision Partners Website

## Project Vision

Build a premium corporate website for **Provision Partners Company Limited** that establishes trust, professionalism, and operational excellence.

The website is **not** an e-commerce platform.

Its primary purpose is to position Provision Partners as a trusted procurement and supply partner for businesses, government institutions, NGOs, and private organizations in Tanzania.

---

# Company Overview

Provision Partners specializes in:

* Office Materials Supply
* Construction Materials Supply
* IT Equipment & Accessories
* Procurement & Supply Services

The website should communicate confidence, reliability, efficiency, and long-term partnership.

---

# Website Objectives

## Primary Objectives

* Generate quotation requests
* Increase brand credibility
* Showcase company capabilities
* Establish a premium online presence
* Support long-term SEO growth

## Secondary Objectives

* Publish company updates
* Share procurement insights
* Highlight industries served
* Present future project portfolio

# Brand Personality

Every visitor should immediately feel:

* Trust
* Professionalism
* Corporate Confidence
* Reliability
* Efficiency
* Operational Excellence

The website should **never** feel like:

* An online store
* A startup landing page
* A SaaS dashboard
* A gaming website
* A portfolio template

# Design Language

The website should feel:

* Premium
* Modern
* Corporate
* Clean
* Spacious
* Elegant

Avoid:

* Excessive gradients
* Heavy glassmorphism
* Neon colors
* Cartoon styling
* Excessive animations
* Cluttered layouts

---

# Typography

Use **Inter** throughout the website.

Typography should emphasize readability and professionalism.

Maintain a clear hierarchy between headings, subheadings, and body text.

---

# Navigation

This website currently follows a **single-page architecture**.

Navigation items should smoothly scroll to their respective sections.

Never navigate to non-existing pages.

Avoid broken links and 404 errors.

Navigation includes:

* Home
* About Us
* Services
* Industries
* Blog
* Contact Us

Primary CTA:

**Request a Quote**

---

# Homepage Structure

The homepage consists of:

2. Hero
3. Trusted By
4. Core Services
5. Industries Served
6. Why Choose Us
7. How We Work

9. Latest From The Blog

---

# Hero Section

The hero is the strongest branding section.

It should communicate:

Reliable Supply Solutions For Modern Businesses

Features:

* Animated content entrance
* Rotating supply showcase
* Professional imagery
* Strong call-to-action buttons

Hero animations should feel elegant rather than flashy.

---

# Cards

All cards across the project must follow one consistent design language.

Requirements:

* Sharp edges
* Consistent shadows
* Consistent borders
* Consistent spacing
* Consistent hover effects

Rounded cards are not allowed unless specifically requested.

---

# Buttons

All buttons must follow one reusable component style.

Requirements:

* Sharp edges
* Consistent height
* Consistent padding
* Consistent typography
* Consistent transitions
* Professional hover animation

Primary Buttons

* Blue background
* White text

Secondary Buttons

* White background
* Blue border
* Blue text

---

# Animations

Animations should communicate quality.

Preferred:

* Fade
* Slide
* Subtle scale
* Smooth scrolling

Avoid:

* Bounce
* Spin
* Flash
* Over-animation

Performance always takes priority over visual effects.

---

# Images

Use realistic business imagery.

Preferred content:

* Office equipment
* IT hardware
* Construction materials
* Procurement operations
* Warehouses
* Corporate logistics
* Professional workplaces

Avoid:

* Cartoon illustrations
* Gaming artwork
* AI fantasy images
* Unrelated stock photography

Use placeholders until approved company assets are available.

---

# Trusted By Section

Use real client logos only when authorized.
